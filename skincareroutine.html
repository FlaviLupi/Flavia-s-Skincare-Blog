The morning and night skincare routines

Added by

.

Morning Skincare Routine

Step 1: Cleanse your face with a cleanser based on your skin type(oily, dry, combined). Step 2: Use a simple and good toner. Step 3: Use natural serums without chemicals. Syep 4: Eye cream if you have eye-bags. Step 5: Apply moisturizer. Step 6: Apply sunscreen to protect your skin from the sun. I usually use the CeraVe products.The products are very good and they a two in one mositurizer and sunscreen so I don't have to use two separate products. (Click here to explore CeraVe products)

Night Skincare Routine

Step 1: Remove your makeup with micellar water,even if you don't have makeup on.I recommend the Nivea Micellar Water. Step 2: Cleanse your face. Step 3: Use toner. Step 4: Use retinol serum to have a healthy young skin. Step 5: Apply eye cream. Step 6: Spot treatment for acne or red bumps. Step 7: Night cream or moisturizer to keep your skin moisturized. Step 8(Optional): Facial oil.
